// skills.txt — Skill Registry for System Awakening
//
// Format:
//   *skill key [rarity] "Label" essence_cost
//     Description text. Can span multiple lines.
//     All indented lines become one description string.
//
// Rarities:
//   • [Rare]     — Common, foundational skills
//   • [Epic]     — Advanced, powerful skills
//   • [Legendary] — Class-defining, ultimate skills
//
// Rules:
//   • key must be a single word (letters, numbers, underscores)
//   • essence_cost is the amount of Essence required to purchase (0 is valid for free skills)
//   • Essence is a unified currency earned from gameplay. Players can spend it on
//     leveling up, purchasing skills, or purchasing items.
//   • description lines must be indented at least one space
//   • a new *skill directive or end of file terminates the previous description
//   • check ownership in scene files with: *check_skill "key" dest_var
//   • grant without Essence cost via:      *grant_skill "key"
//   • remove a skill via:                  *revoke_skill "key"

// SHIELD OF HUMANITY CLASS SKILLS
// Central (Class-Defining) Skills

*skill last_shield_standing [Legendary] "Last Shield Standing" 0
  You are the final guardian, the ultimate defense against extinction. You may designate yourself as the unbreakable shield against a specific threat. Once designated, that threat cannot advance past you. Attacks directed at anyone behind you are reduced in effectiveness by 50%—the threat may still damage those you protect, but the burden falls on you, not them. You gain the ability to intercept any attack directed at a protected ally and take it instead, instantly teleporting to the source if necessary. As long as you stand, the threat you've designated cannot reach those behind you. This is absolute. Every time you block an attack intended for another, you gain one "Shield Mark." At 100 Shield Marks, you reach Critical Protection—your defensive capabilities quintuple but your offensive power is reduced to 10%. At 200 Shield Marks, you enter a state of pure defense, unable to attack but literally immovable. At 300 Shield Marks, you achieve perfect sacrifice: you become immortal and invulnerable, but your time is measured in minutes. Shield Marks decrease at 1 per minute of rest.

*skill last_bastion [Legendary] "Last Bastion" 0
  When all other defenses fail and the darkness closes in, you have one final refuge. You cast Last Bastion to create an underground bunker—a true stronghold designed to survive a siege. The [System] instantly locates the safest possible location within 1 mile of your current position. You and up to 10 × this skill's level can teleport to the bunker when activated. Only designated initial entrants may come and go freely. The entrance is a subway-like stairwell with a 15-foot diameter circle, automatically placed where monsters are least likely to find it. The stairwell contains runes that destroy any monster that enters. Below is a 20-foot tunnel with offensive and defensive enchantments, ending in a reinforced blast door. The main chamber is 10 feet tall with 1,000 square feet per person brought with you. Fully stocked with food, water, recycled air, and supplies for up to six months. Costs 2,000,000 mana from a separate pool that fills at 5% of your max mana per hour when your mana is full. Others can contribute mana or use mana stones to accelerate charging. Cooldown: One year after casting.

// Standard Skills

*skill interpenetration [Epic] "Interpenetration" 0
  You move faster toward those in danger. Whenever an ally is threatened or about to take damage, gain +100% movement speed toward them and may instantly move to intercept the threat, teleporting if necessary. Once you reach the threatened ally, gain +50% damage reduction for 5 seconds. This skill has no cooldown—use it as many times as threats emerge. You cannot be slowed or hindered while moving to protect an ally, and you automatically path around obstacles. The [System] itself bends slightly to get you where you need to be.

*skill unbreakable [Legendary] "Unbreakable" 0
  A passive skill representing your supernatural durability. Gain +1% physical defense for every ally currently within 50 meters of you. Also gain bonus health equal to 50% of your Fortitude stat for every person who depends on you (tracked by your class, up to 100 people). Take 20% reduced damage from all sources while allies are alive and behind you. If an ally dies while you were nearby, gain temporary +30% damage and defense for the next 60 seconds as grief transforms into wrath.

*skill absorbent [Epic] "Absorbent" 0
  You absorb incoming damage and convert it into protection. Every point of damage you take heals you for 5% of that damage as the [System] recognizes your sacrifice. After taking 1,000 cumulative damage, gain a temporary shield equal to 25% of that damage, which persists until broken. Any status effects, curses, or debuffs applied to nearby allies can be transferred to you instead. You take the injury in their place. Gain immunity to most negative effects (diseases, poisons, curses) as your body hardens from accumulated suffering.

*skill defiant_stand [Rare] "Defiant Stand" 0
  You plant yourself and refuse to move. For up to 60 seconds, gain +80% damage reduction and cannot be displaced, knocked back, or moved by any force except death itself. While in this stance, allies behind you gain +40% damage reduction as well. At the end of the stance, you may release all absorbed damage as a shockwave that pushes back all threats 30 meters. 45-second cooldown after releasing the stance.

*skill intervention [Epic] "Intervention" 0
  Passive skill representing your supernatural awareness of danger. Sense threats to nearby allies 3 seconds before they strike. You automatically move to intercept lethal attacks directed at allies. Once per combat, if an ally would be killed by an attack, you can instantly appear between them and the attack, taking the full damage instead. This skill triggers automatically, requiring no action on your part. The [System] recognizes protection as your fundamental nature and assists you.

*skill regeneration_of_purpose [Rare] "Regeneration of Purpose" 0
  Your healing scales directly with how many people depend on you. Gain health regeneration equal to 5% of your maximum health per minute for every conscious ally within 100 meters, up to 100% (one full health bar per minute with enough allies nearby). If all allies die or become incapacitated, your health regeneration drops to 5% immediately and decreases by 1% every 10 seconds. You may choose to share your regeneration with allies; any health you would regenerate can be redirected to nearby allies instead.

*skill unwavering_conviction [Epic] "Unwavering Conviction" 0
  A passive skill making you increasingly difficult to defeat the more allies you protect. For every conscious ally within 100 meters, gain +2% to all stats. Additionally, gain +1% to all stats for every permanent Shield Mark you carry (even after they fade, the scars remain). You cannot be feared or demoralized, especially when allies depend on you. Attempts to break your will or make you abandon those you protect automatically fail.

*skill final_moment [Legendary] "Final Moment" 0
  When you reach critical health (below 25% maximum), this skill automatically activates. You enter a hyper-protective state where you gain triple damage reduction, all damage dealt to you heals nearby allies for 25% of the damage, you move 50% faster toward threatened allies, and any attack that would kill you is blocked once (after which the attacker faces your counterattack at triple damage). When you fall while in this state, there's a final burst of protection: all allies within 50 meters gain +50% to all stats and full health regeneration for 60 seconds after you die.

*skill legacy_of_protection [Legendary] "Legacy of Protection" 0
  Passive skill ensuring your sacrifice matters beyond your own death. Any location where you've successfully protected someone becomes slightly safer permanently. Corruption spreads 50% slower in places you've protected. Creatures born in places you've died from are inherently weaker and less willing to prey on the weak. Anyone who witnesses you protecting others gains inspiration and permanent +5% to all stats as long as they remember you (multiple witnesses stack this bonus). If you die protecting your sanctuary or a large group, that location becomes sacred for one year—anything created there is 200% more effective, and any healing is doubled.
