*category core

*skill last_shield_standing [Legendary] "Last Shield Standing" 0
  Every time you stood between others and danger, this skill took shape. 
  Description: Designate a single threat: creature, force, or group. While active, that threat cannot advance past your position. Attacks aimed at allies behind you redirect to you; you teleport to intercept if necessary.
  Choose one of three modes:
  Sentinel: Your defense quadruples. Your attack halves.
  Bastion: You cannot attack. You cannot be moved or displaced by any means.
  Bulwark: You become invulnerable for 0.5x skill level seconds.
  Activation cost: 500 mana, with 10 mana per second upkeep.
  Switching modes costs an additional 50 mana.

*skill last_bastion [Legendary] "Last Bastion" 0
  When the horde pressed in and retreat meant death for those behind you, you held the line. Bastions have served the same purpose for thousands of years: they are the places where the desperate make their stand, where supplies outlast siege engines, where time becomes a weapon. This skill is the answer to that ancient need.
  Description: Creates an underground bunker at the safest location within 1 mile, chosen by the [System]. The entrance is a 15-foot stairwell that destroys any non-human intruder. Below it, an enchanted tunnel ends at a reinforced blast door. The chamber beyond provides 1,000 square feet per person, stocked with food, water, recycled air, and medical supplies for six months. 
  Capacity: 10 per skill level. Only those you designate at the time of casting may enter and exit freely.
  The bunker draws from a separate and dedicated mana reserve that charges at the speed of your mana regeneration, but only when your personal mana pool is full. Allies may contribute their mana directly to the reserve, and mana stones may be used to add up to 1,000,000 mana per year.
  Activation cost: 2,000,000 mana from dedicated reserve only.
  Cooldown: 1 year.

*category active

*skill interpenetration [Epic] "Interpenetration" 0
  When an ally is about to take damage, your movement speed doubles toward them and you may teleport to their position. On arrival, you gain +50% damage reduction for 5 seconds. You cannot be slowed or obstructed while moving to protect an ally.
  Mana Cost: 100 per activation.

*skill defiant_stand [Rare] "Defiant Stand" 0
  Take a stance and hold your ground. For up to 60 seconds, you gain +80% damage reduction and become immune to displacement, knockback, and forced repositioning. When the stance ends, all absorbed damage is released as a shockwave that pushes every threat within 30 meters away from you.
  Mana Cost: 200 to activate. 20 per second after the first 10 seconds.
  Cooldown: 45 seconds after releasing the stance.

*category passive

*skill final_moment [Legendary] "Final Moment" 0
  When your health drops below 25%, you automatically gain triple damage reduction and +50% movement speed. The next single attack that would reduce you to 0 health is blocked; you immediately counter for triple your normal damage. This state persists until the triggering threat is eliminated or you reach 0 health.

*skill unbreakable [Legendary] "Unbreakable" 0
  Gain +1% physical defense per ally currently within 50 meters (max +50%). Gain bonus maximum health equal to 50% of your Fortitude stat per person who depends on you, as tracked by your class (max 100 people). When an ally within 50 meters is killed, gain +30% damage and defense for 60 seconds.

*skill legacy_of_protection [Legendary] "Legacy of Protection" 0
  Any location where you have held ground against a threat during a conflict becomes permanently altered: corruption there spreads 50% more slowly, and monsters native to that area are moderately weakened. Any person who directly witnesses you take damage to protect another gains +5% to all stats permanently (stacks per witness, no cap). If you die while actively shielding others, the location is marked for one year — all crafting and healing performed there is twice as effective.

*skill unwavering_conviction [Epic] "Unwavering Conviction" 0
  Gain +2% to all stats for each conscious ally within 100 meters. Gain an additional +1% to all stats per permanent Shield Mark scar — these persist even after active Marks fade. You are immune to fear, demoralization, and any compulsion to abandon those you protect.

*skill absorbent [Epic] "Absorbent" 0
  Each point of damage you take heals you for 5% of that amount. After absorbing 1,000 cumulative damage, gain a temporary damage shield equal to 25% of that total; the shield persists until broken, then the counter resets. You may choose to absorb a status effect, curse, or debuff targeting a nearby ally, transferring it to yourself. Accumulated exposure has left you immune to disease, poison, and curses.

*skill intervention [Epic] "Intervention" 0
  You sense threats to nearby allies approximately 3 seconds before they strike, giving you time to reposition. You automatically move to block attacks that would be lethal to an ally. Once per combat, if an ally would be reduced to 0 health by a single hit, you instantly appear between them and the attack and absorb the full damage yourself.

*skill regeneration_of_purpose [Rare] "Regeneration of Purpose" 0
  Gain health regeneration equal to 5% of your maximum health per minute for each conscious ally within 100 meters, up to a maximum of 100% per minute. If every ally within 100 meters is incapacitated or dead, your regeneration drops immediately to 5% and decreases by 1% every 10 seconds until it reaches a minimum.
